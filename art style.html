Art style

-The original "Space Impact" (a game for the ZX Spectrum) has a chi-graphical, minimalist, and pixelated art style reminiscent of early 8-bit game aesthetics, focusing on essential shapes and vibrant colors to represent the alien environment. -Pixelated Graphics: -The game's graphics are rendered using individual pixels, giving it a distinct and retro look. -Enemies, objects, and the player's ship are represented by basic, easily recognizable shapes. -Space Impact" utilized a limited but effective color palette with a few key colors to create visual distinction
